Resilient Income Improvement

ZAMANGRIC CAPITAL CORP seeks, among other things, to improve incomes, resilience of the informal sector, and farmers and small-scale entrepreneurs by increasing production and productivity across selected sectors.

ZAMANGRIC CAPITAL CORP was established by a group of Zambian visionaries and entrepreneurs to use profitable enterprise as a vehicle for significantly contributing to the socio-economic dynamics of Zambia.

Investment

ZAMANGRIC CAPITAL CORP has planned investments of over five years (2023-2035) for phase one interventions to accelerate the development of financial inclusion through its commercializing small-scale farmers through the implementation of an agriculture, food processing marketable and sustainable 7 agricultural value chains in Zambia.
