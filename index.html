About Us!

At ZAMANGRIC CAPITAL CORP, we specialize in connecting government, industry, and community to provide equity-funding capital for infrastructure & Agriculture projects over $1Billion. Our seasoned partners have long-term business relationships. These relationships have resulted in Global partnership opportunities.

ZAMANGRIC CAPITAL CORP focuses on strategic alliances in the infrastructure development sector. Mainly, in providing a better solution for governments than the current P3 model, demonstrating sustainable and predictable cash flows.
